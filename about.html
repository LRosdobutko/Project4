Why the Fantasy Bestiary?

This assignment rapidly grew in scope until it became something I wanted to continue after submission

Sometimes you run out of ideas for yourself, and you need a quick fix to get you going.

A crowd-sourced site for monsters, and their stats is an excellent way to bring some chaos to table top or video gaming.

The images utilized on this site were generated with a free AI art generator: Perchance
