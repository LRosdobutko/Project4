Beasts, Monsters, Demonds and Fiends!

The Fantasy Monster Bestiary is the one stop shop for free fantasy monster ideas.

Add your own, or choose from a list of monsters to populate your RPG or table top game.

In order to add your own monster you will have to contact us with the information you would like to use to create your monster, so that we can verify your creation before submitting it to the database.

Click the name of a monster on the left to view it's stats, and descriptions, as well as an image that can be used for reference.

The images utilized on this site were generated with a free AI art generator: Perchance
